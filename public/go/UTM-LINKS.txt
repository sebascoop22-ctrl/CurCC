Cooper Concierge — UTM landing redirects (/go/)

Use these in Instagram / TikTok / etc. bios and posts.

Base pattern (production):
  https://www.cooperconcierge.co.uk/go/<platform>

Per-person tracking (later):
  .../go/instagram?ref=jane   → utm_content=jane
  .../go/tiktok?ref=marcus

Campaign variant (default campaign is "bio"):
  .../go/instagram?c=stories

GA4 reads: Session source = utm_source, medium = utm_medium, campaign = utm_campaign,
and utm_content when ?ref= is used.

Platforms: instagram, tiktok, facebook, youtube, linkedin
